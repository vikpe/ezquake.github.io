Installing ezQuake

Follow these steps to get started with ezQuake.

1. Buy Quake on Steam
Quake is required in order to run ezQuake. This will give you the complete single-player game.

2. Download ezQuake (full install)
Extract the content to your Quake installation directory.

nQuake = ezQuake + goodies

To get configured for competitive online games quickly, try nQuake.

nQuake includes config files, modern HUD, fullbright skins and 24-bit textures. Once nQuake is installed, download an 'Upgrade' version of ezQuake and replace the executable in your Quake directory.
